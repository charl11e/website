About me

Hello! My name is Charlie and I am currently a 2nd year Maths and Computer Science student at the University of Liverpool. Before I get into the details of my degree, I'd love an opportunity to tell you a bit about myself:

Ever since I've been at high school, I always loved both Maths and Computer Science, and having an opportunity to do a joint honours degree in both subjects was a perfect fit for me. In fact, it has made me appreciate both subjects at a deeper level because fundamentally, Computer Science is heavily reliant on Maths. Just taking one particular (but relevant) example, the transformer model in machine learning, which underpins many advancements in generative AI, is heavily reliant on linear algebra, and in particular, matrix multiplication. While this is just one of many, many examples, being able to appreciate technologies such as this from both a mathematical and computer science viewpoint is what I love most about my degree.

I have experience in a variety of programming languages, including Python, Javascript and Java. I also have experience with HTML, CSS and node.js. Currently, my main focus is on Java programming as it is the primary language used in my degree. You can find a variety of projects on my projects page, which includes projects I have worked on as part of my degree, as well as personal projects.
